About us

Gas & Oil Trading is a leading North American energy producer that is focused on growing its strong portfolio of diverse resource plays producing natural gas, oil and natural gas liquids. By partnering with employees, community organizations and other businesses, we contribute to the strength and sustainability of the communities where we operate.

Our management team is comprised of knowledgeable industry professionals. In fact, most of our team members have more than 20 years of experience in the oil and gas industry.

About us

We will always be on the lookout for the best rocks and we will work to identify a limited number of core growth assets characterized by high returns, scale and running room. Our strategy is centered on diversifying our commodity mix and growing value in top tier assets.
